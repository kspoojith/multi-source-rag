తెలంగాణ రాష్ట్రం - Telangana State
======================================

తెలంగాణ భారతదేశంలోని 29వ రాష్ట్రం. 2 జూన్ 2014న ఆంధ్రప్రదేశ్ నుండి
విడిపోయి ప్రత్యేక రాష్ట్రంగా ఏర్పడింది.

Telangana is the 29th state of India, formed on 2 June 2014 by
bifurcation of Andhra Pradesh. Hyderabad serves as its capital.

Key Facts:
- Capital: Hyderabad (హైదరాబాద్)
- Area: 1,12,077 sq km
- Population: approximately 3.5 crore (2011 census)
- Districts: 33 (after reorganization)
- Official Language: Telugu (తెలుగు)
- State Animal: Deer (Jinka - జింక)
- State Bird: Palapitta (Indian Roller - పాలపిట్ట)

Hyderabad - IT Hub:
Hyderabad is home to HITEC City, one of India's largest IT hubs.
Major companies like Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Apple, and Facebook
have their offices here. The city is also known as "Cyberabad."

హైదరాబాద్ లో HITEC City ఉంది, ఇది భారతదేశంలోని అతిపెద్ద IT హబ్‌లలో ఒకటి.

Historical Places:
1. Charminar (చార్మినార్) - Built in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah
2. Golconda Fort (గోల్కొండ కోట) - Famous diamond fort
3. Warangal Fort (వరంగల్ కోట) - Kakatiya dynasty
4. Ramappa Temple - UNESCO World Heritage Site (2021)

Economy:
- IT and ITES sector is the largest contributor
- Pharma hub: Genome Valley in Hyderabad
- Agriculture: Rice, cotton, turmeric are major crops
- Telangana contributes about 5% to India's GDP

Telangana Movement:
The Telangana movement for a separate state lasted over 60 years.
The movement gained momentum after K. Chandrashekar Rao's (KCR)
fast unto death in 2009. The Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act
was passed by Indian Parliament in February 2014.
