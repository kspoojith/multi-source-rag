Narendra Modi - Prime Minister of India
========================================

Narendra Damodardas Modi was born on 17 September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat.
He is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India, serving since 26 May 2014.

Education:
Modi completed his higher secondary education in Vadnagar. He earned a degree
in political science from the University of Delhi through distance education.
He later completed a Master of Arts (MA) in Political Science from Gujarat
University in 1983.

Early Life:
As a child, Modi helped his father sell tea at the Vadnagar railway station.
He was introduced to the RSS (Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh) at the age of 8.

Political Career:
- 2001-2014: Chief Minister of Gujarat (longest serving CM of Gujarat)
- 2014-present: Prime Minister of India
- He led BJP to a historic majority in the 2014 general elections
- Won re-election in 2019 with an even larger majority

Key Initiatives:
1. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission) - launched 2 October 2014
2. Make in India - to boost manufacturing sector
3. Digital India - for digital empowerment
4. Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana - financial inclusion scheme
5. Ayushman Bharat - world's largest healthcare programme
6. Startup India - to promote entrepreneurship

International Relations:
Modi has been recognized for strengthening India's foreign relations.
He has visited over 60 countries and hosted numerous world leaders.
India's relationship with the US, Japan, and Israel has strengthened
significantly under his leadership.
